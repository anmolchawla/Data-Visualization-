UN World Population Data

A visual analysis of world population data

The 2017 Revision of World Population Prospects represents the latest global set of demographic estimates and projections prepared by the Population Division of the Department of Economic and Social Affairs of the United Nations Secretariat. Ten countries were selected, a variety of counrties were taken into consideration ranging from densely populated to saprsely populated. The data consists of ten countries as follows

China
France
India
Portugal
Singapore
Spain
Sweden
Switzerland
United Kingdom
United States of America

The data has been collected from 1978 to 2018, at an interval of ten years and the values are in thousands and the metric for the population is the highest number of people recorded in the country in the given year disregarding other variables like migration etc.
The X-axis is representative of the years, Y-axis is represnentative of the population numbers in thousands and the lines with differnt colours are respresntativve of the countries as shown in the legend.
